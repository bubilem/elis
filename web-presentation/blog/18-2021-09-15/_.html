Today was the most eventfull out of all the days, since we arrived in Karlskrona, Sweden. The sunset from the boat was very pretty so we went to take some pictures and then unloaded off the ship. The ship was a big freight ship, as you can see on the picture. After unloading, we got onto our bus and made a few stops around the city, taking pictures wherever we could. We also visited a naval museum full of swedish naval history. After Karlskrona came Kalmar, which was comprised of both historic and modern structures. We visited a local castle, used for the signing of a pact of scandinavian countries in the 15th century. There was also the castle town, made out of very small and brightly colored houses. It even had its own dwarf settlement! After sightseeing came shopping. The day ended by us getting onto another freight ship and sailing home.
